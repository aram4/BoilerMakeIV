About NinjaShop

NinjaShop is a free web application created by 4 college students, Amer Alsabbagah, Sarthak Anand, Achintya Ram, and Rajiv Samagond at BoilerMake 2017. It is designed to allow customers to save time and effort during a shopping spree by being able to input their desired list of items and follow an optimal path through the shop.

NinjaShop is partnered with several malls, grocery stores, and retailers around the United States, including Jewel Osco, Costco, and Walmart.
